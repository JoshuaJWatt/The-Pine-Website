The Pineapple Family

My binomial name is Ananas Comosus. I am a tropical plant with an edible fruit and is the most economically significant plant in the family Bromeliaceae.


We pineapples take about 18-20 months to become ready to harvest. Pineapples are native to South America before Christopher Columbus discovered us in 1493.
